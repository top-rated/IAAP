Therapeutic groups

Analytical group of personal experience

The main difference between Analytical Group of Personal Experience and Personal Growth Groups is that Personal Growth Groups are aimed at the development of personality or its certain qualities. And the priority of the Analytical Group of Personal Experience is a better understanding of oneself and one's behavior in the conditions of interaction in the group.
