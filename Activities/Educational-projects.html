Educational projects

Author's course "Homeopathy: the path from literal to symbolic"

In ancient times, any ailment, disease, emotional experience was considered a divine act and only God or some other divine act could heal a person. A necessary condition for healing is awareness and an appropriate attitude towards the disease. This is how homeopathy works.

Analytical intervention group

Day analytical group in Poznyaki. The start of the group's work is February 4. The group is planned to be held twice a month: on Tuesdays. The time is from 14:00 to 17:30, including a 20-minute break.

Two-year training program of the Sandplay method

We invite psychologists and psychotherapists to participate in the two-year training program of the Sandplay method. Sandplay (sand therapy) is an in-depth method of psychological work that combines the principles of Jungian analysis and play therapy and is used with adults, children, groups and families.

Course "Christian Mysticism of Medieval Europe"

The course "Christian Mysticism of Medieval Europe" examines in detail the development of the phenomenon of mysticism in its Christian version on the example of European medieval spiritual thought. Special emphasis is placed on symbolism and metaphorical language and its significance for the conscious and unconscious life of the European soul. The course materials were successfully tested and completed during its full implementation for the Plato's Cave educational project (September 2020 - March 2021). The course is held on the ZOOM online platform.

Specialization course: "Analytical psychology and the basics of modern psychoanalysis" - Online version, aimed at colleagues from other cities.

This course is a combination of lectures and practical classes, in which, with the help of demonstrations, exercises and clinical supervision, students have the opportunity to learn classical and author's analytical methods, created both from within Jungian analysis and in the fields of modern psychoanalysis.

Educational program "Introduction to Analytical Psychology"

Modern challenges, such as the loss of social ties, the increase in the speed of information transmission, the constant migration of the population, the loss of the meaning of existence return us to the search for the Soul, and therefore increase the relevance of Carl Gustav Jung's analytical psychology. We invite students and graduates of psychological, philosophical, philological, historical, and medical faculties to participate in the "Introduction to Analytical Psychology" program.

Educational program for beginners "Children's Analytical Psychotherapy"

The program will present material in the form of lectures in combination with examples from clinical practice, exercises and role plays. This format allows, based on experience and knowledge, to understand the inner world of the child and master the initial skills of therapeutic work with children.

Training program with Sandplay therapy

We invite you to the educational program with Sandplay therapy "Edges of Sandplay". Sand therapy is a deep psychological method that originated in the middle of the 20th century. Currently, it is successfully used by various psychological schools with adults, children, groups and families. We teach the method - you use it according to your theoretical base.

Educational program "Analytical psychology and the foundations of modern psychoanalysis"

The offered course is a combination of lectures and practical classes, in which, with the help of demonstrations, exercises and clinical supervision, students have the opportunity to learn classical and original analytical methods, created both from within Jungian analysis and in the fields of modern psychoanalysis.

The program "Narcissism as a mirror of the inner world"

The Jungian program "Alchemy of the Soul" invites you to explore the theme of Season I "Narcissism as a mirror of the inner world" and delve into its individual aspects in ten two-day workshops.

Observation of babies according to the Esther Bick method

Baby Observation by Esther Beek is a training project for analytically oriented psychologists and psychotherapists who work or plan to work with children and/or adults.
