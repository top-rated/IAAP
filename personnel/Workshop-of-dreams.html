Workshop of dreams

We invite you every Monday to the "Dream Workshop" daytime group, which started on January 27 from 10:00 a.m. to 1:00 p.m. at the address: str. Drahomanova, 2.

Such an unusual beginning of the week will allow you to gather your thoughts and feelings, put your inner world in order and emerge into reality with its details and tasks in a clearer, more conscious state. Release strength and energy for life.

Leading programs:

Angela Timshina is an analytically oriented psychologist, psychotherapist, member of the Ukrainian Development Group of the IAAP. Systemic family therapist, member of USP (educational trainer of USP);
Anna Kovalenko is an analytically oriented psychologist, psychotherapist, arranger.

The cost of one meeting is UAH 400. Missed meetings are NOT paid for, because the group is open and will be closed at the request of the participants.
