About us

The Ukrainian IAAP development group was created for the purpose of professional development of psychologists and psychotherapists and the organization of training in analytical psychology according to international standards. Analytical psychology originated in the works of the Swiss psychiatrist Carl Gustav Jung (1875-1961). Offering a comprehensive model of the human psyche, analytical psychology includes a psychotherapeutic approach to improving mental health and facilitating personality maturation, as well as a theoretical set of knowledge with the possibility of wide application to social and cultural issues.

The Ukrainian Development Group of the IAAP has an associate membership institute for individuals who seek to develop in the key of analytical psychology in order to grow in a conscious understanding of psychological reality in themselves and society, find healing and meaning in their lives, achieve greater depth in their relationships and build a life in accordance with a found sense of purpose.
